Workshop Awards

Recognizing outstanding contributions to geometric foundation models

Best Paper Award

Recognizing exceptional research contributions

The Best Paper Award recognizes the most innovative and impactful research presented at the GeoFM workshop. Winners are selected based on novelty, technical quality, potential impact on the field, and presentation quality. The selection committee consists of senior researchers in geometric deep learning and foundation models.

Award Criteria

Innovation

Novel approaches to incorporating geometric principles into foundation models or creative applications of geometric deep learning to scientific problems.

Technical Quality

Rigorous methodology, sound theoretical foundations, and thorough experimental validation of the proposed approach.

Impact Potential

Potential to significantly advance the field of geometric foundation models or make meaningful contributions to scientific discovery.

Best Review Award

Recognizing exceptional peer review contributions

The Best Review Award honors reviewers who provided exceptionally thoughtful, constructive, and insightful feedback during the paper review process. This award acknowledges the critical role of peer review in maintaining scientific quality and fostering community growth. The selection is based on review quality, depth of analysis, and helpfulness to authors.

Review Quality Metrics

Depth & Insight

Reviews that demonstrate deep understanding of the technical content and provide meaningful insights beyond surface-level comments.

Constructive Feedback

Reviews that offer specific, actionable suggestions for improvement rather than merely pointing out flaws.

Timeliness & Thoroughness

Reviews submitted promptly that demonstrate careful reading and consideration of the entire manuscript.

Award Ceremony Details

The award ceremony will take place during the closing session of the GeoFM workshop on July 20, 2025, from 4:00 PM to 4:30 PM. All award recipients will be notified by June 30, 2025, and invited to prepare a brief acceptance speech (maximum 2 minutes).

In addition to recognition at the workshop, all award recipients will receive:

  • Best Paper Award: $1,000 honorarium for first place, $500 for second place, $250 for honorable mention
  • Best Review Award: $750 honorarium for first place, $350 for second place
  • Digital certificates signed by the workshop organizers and sponsors
  • Highlighted presentation of their work on the workshop website and social media channels

For questions regarding the awards program, please contact: awards@geofm-workshop.org
