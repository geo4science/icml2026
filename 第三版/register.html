Register for GeoFM Workshop

Join us for the Workshop on Geometric Foundation Models at ICML 2025. Registration is free but required for attendance. By registering, you'll gain access to all workshop sessions, poster presentations, and the opportunity to network with leading researchers in geometric deep learning and foundation models.

Full Workshop Access

Attend all keynote talks, paper presentations, and panel discussions throughout the day.

Networking

Connect with researchers during coffee breaks, lunch, and dedicated poster sessions.

Workshop Proceedings

Access to all accepted papers and presentation slides after the workshop.

By registering, you agree to our Privacy Policy and Code of Conduct. Your information will only be used for workshop communications and planning purposes.

Important Registration Notes

  • This workshop registration is separate from ICML 2025 conference registration. You must be registered for ICML 2025 to attend this workshop.
  • Registration is limited to the first 200 participants due to venue capacity constraints.
  • Registered participants will receive a confirmation email with additional details one week before the workshop.
  • Cancellations must be submitted at least 48 hours before the workshop to free up your spot for others.
